 Is there a place to test out the Vista V3 before purchasing?

Definitely! Please find our list of authorized retailers here who have the Vista V3 available for you to try. Our Hub service center and showrooms also contain our full line of products and accessories so you can test and try before you buy!

 Do you need an adapter to use an infant car seat on the Vista V3 stroller?

You do not need an adapter to use any UPPAbaby branded infant car seats or bassinet, they will directly attach to the Vista V3 stroller. For other non-UPPAbaby infant car seats we offer adapters to connect to the stroller.

 Does the Vista V3 go through any chemical testing?

Yes, the Vista V3 is Green Guard Certified which means it’s been tested and evaluated above and beyond the government standards to meet stringent chemical emissions limits of more than 360 airborne chemicals. In addition to our high chemical standards, the leather used on our stroller is REACH compliant certified testing for over 200+ chemicals.Visit the Our Planet page to learn more.

 What’s the difference between the Cruz and the Vista?

The Vista is the ultimate full featured single to double stroller designed to grow with families and can accommodate up to 3 children. It is great for families who want to plan for the future growth of their family.The Cruz is the optimal choice for families looking for a single, full-size stroller that can handle city walks while remaining compact for storage. You can add a ride along board to stroll with 2 but it can not take a second seat like the Vista.

 Does the Vista stroller fold with the Toddler Seat?

Yes, you can fold the Vista with the Toddler Seat attached. The Toddler Seat must be forward-facing. For a more compact fold, recline the Toddler Seat to the 2nd most upright position. Click here for a how-to video.
