### Ready for Every Trip.™

 Ideal for jet-setting and everyday outings, the Minu V3 is your go-to, from-birth travel stroller. Transition easily with its one-handed, overhead-friendly compact fold, and give your little one the best ride with its enhanced comfort features. 

![Image 60: GreenGuard Gold Certification](https://back.uppababy.com/media/7d/1b/c6/1759777181/Icon_GGG_32x32.svg)
GREENGUARD® Gold Certification

*   Portable and the perfect fit with an intuitive, one-handed fold and unfold and IATA compatible dimensions
*   Accommodates from infant to toddler with a multi-positional footrest, integrated foot barrier, a deep recline, and an adjustable, 5-point, no-rethread harness.
*   All-wheel suspension with independent rear suspension for a smooth ride for both child and caregiver
*   Enhanced comfort with a padded, integrated insert for more plush coziness and a high seat back for children up to 50 lbs
*   3-Year Warranty

### Selected FAQs ([View All Product FAQs](https://support.uppababy.com/hc/en-us/sections/30600886926615-Minu-V3))

 How do you fold the Minu V3?

The Minu V3 can be folded with one hand. Below are the steps to fold: Engage the brake. Simultaneously slide and squeeze grey button on handlebar to initiate fold. Ensure safety latch engages on the frame. Note: Use built-in handle to transport. Click here for a how-to video.

 Does the bumper bar and snack tray fold with the Minu V3 stroller?

Yes, after engaging each side button with one hand and adjusting the accessory up, you are able to fold both the bumper bar and the snack tray with the Minu V3.

 How easy is the Minu V3 to maneuver?

The Minu V3 has all wheel suspension making it easy to maneuver over multiple terrains throughout your day to day errands.

 Does the Minu V3 go through any chemical testing?

Yes, the Minu V3 is Green Guard Certified which means it’s been tested and evaluated above and beyond the government standards to meet stringent chemical emissions limits of more than 360 airborne chemicals. In addition to our high chemical standards, the leather used on our stroller is REACH compliant certified testing for over 200+ chemicals. To learn more, please visit https://uppababy.com/our-company/our-planet/

 Does the Minu V3 fit in an overhead?

Yes! Our Minu V3 is compliant with IATA, which means its folded dimensions meet the requirements for carry-on luggage (22” x 18” x 10”). We always recommend checking with your airline prior to your trip as carry-on allowance may vary from airline to airline.
