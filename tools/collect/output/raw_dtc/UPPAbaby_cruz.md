### Selected FAQs ([View All Product FAQs](https://support.uppababy.com/hc/en-us/sections/33157806822167-Cruz-V3))

 What’s the difference between the Cruz V3 and the Vista V3?

The Cruz V3 is our streamlined, full-size stroller designed to grow with your child from day one and can accommodate up to 2 children. It’s perfect for families focusing on the present, planning for one child, or with a wider age gap between siblings.The Vista V3 is the ultimate full featured single to double stroller designed to grow with families and can accommodate up to 3 children. It is great for families who want to plan for the future growth of their family. 

 Can I add a second seat on the Cruz V3?

No, the Cruz V3 is a single stroller and does not support a second seat. However, you can add the PiggyBack ride-along board for an older sibling.

 Is the Bassinet V3 compatible with the Cruz V3?

Yes, the Cruz V3 is compatible with the Bassinet V3, offering another from-birth option in addition to the included toddler seat with full recline.

 Is there a place to test out the Cruz V3 before purchasing?
