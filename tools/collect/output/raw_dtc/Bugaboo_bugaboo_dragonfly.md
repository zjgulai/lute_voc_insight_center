What are the key features of the Bugaboo Dragonfly stroller?What are the key features of the Bugaboo Dragonfly stroller?

 If you're an urban parent who's looking for a compact and lightweight city stroller that is easy to use, the Bugaboo Dragonfly is a perfect choice.

 The flexible storage system offers 22 lbs of storage and includes an expandable bag that can be moved to the rear, front, or on the handlebar. You'll have all the room you need for life, right from day one. 

Is the Bugaboo Dragonfly suitable for newborns?Is the Bugaboo Dragonfly suitable for newborns?

 Yes. If you purchase the Bugaboo Dragonfly with a bassinet and a seat, your newborn can lie flat and enjoy comfortable strolls from the very first day. The padded bassinet comes with a breezy panel and an aerated mattress, which helps your baby cool down during hotter days. And when it gets chilly, you can close the panel for warmth. Alternatively, you can also pair the stroller with a compatible car seat, such as the Bugaboo Turtle Air by Nuna, and make it suitable from birth. 

 The Dragonfly is also future-proof. When your newborn grows up into a curious toddler, just switch from the bassinet to the seat. 

How do I fold and unfold the Bugaboo Dragonfly?How do I fold and unfold the Bugaboo Dragonfly?

 The patented design in the Bugaboo Dragonfly lets you fold it with one hand without removing the bassinet or seat. Here's how you do it: 

 Step 1: Activate the brake by stepping on the brake pedal.

 Step 2: Bring the handlebar down using the large button until the handlebar reaches the fold stripe.

 Step 3: Press the large button while sliding the fold button to whichever side you prefer.

 Step 4: Lift the handlebar upwards and use a bit of momentum to bring the front of the stroller towards you. The seat or bassinet will fold down automatically.

 Unfolding it is just as simple and quick: 

 Step 1: Press the big button while sliding the fold button to whichever side you prefer.

 Step 2: Lift the handlebar upwards, then pull it towards you and down. This will separate the front and rear wheels and open the stroller. Click the handlebar in place by pushing down until you hear a click.

 Step 3: If you have a seat, pull the seat upwards using the footrest or back rest. Note: Do not use the seat recline button. If you have a bassinet, pull the bassinet strap towards you to open the bassinet. Pull it until you hear a click. You're good to go.
