Under the Consumer Rights Act 2015, if a product is faulty within the first six months, customers are entitled to a refund, repair, or replacement, yet Elvie tried to dismiss their responsibility.

I had to pay for new pumps out of pocket and even had to rush out just before shops closed when my faulty Elvie pumps failed unexpectedly. No mum should have to deal with this level of stress, inconvenience, and financial loss over a product that was supposed to be covered under warranty.

Elvie’s refusal to uphold basic consumer rights and their shocking lack of customer support make them a company to avoid. If you expect reliable products or fair treatment under UK consumer law, do not buy from Elvie.
