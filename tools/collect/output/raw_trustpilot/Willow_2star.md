[## Great experience for 3 months only](https://www.trustpilot.com/reviews/6473431e0de5c0c7d1e5cb1f)
Great experience for 3 months. App seems like a useless feature for me. My guess is that the Go is built on the same platform as the fancier pulp that has more data collected in the app. Then right when returning to work it stopped properly pumping. It never worked right after that despite update after update and new replacement parts. I’ve wasted lots of my precious time trying to troubleshoot with their representatives, including receiving survey after survey on how the service was for emails they never responded to. Calling got me someone but enough time had passed since the last troubleshooting session that I needed an update once again. None of the updates fixed the problem, and, despite its age, my Freemie pump IS consistent and reliable. Very disappointed that something that was supposed to make my life easier made it harder in the end.
