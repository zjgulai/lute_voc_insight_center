[## Awful Experience With Pumps and Support](https://www.trustpilot.com/reviews/68b70b73eea5c47f2c51d340)

Within 6 weeks of purchase, one pump started clicking and shutting off randomly. Then the second one followed suit. Now I’m spending nearly every session hitting the pumps on my chest just to get a few seconds of suction at a time. I’ve done every single troubleshooting step they suggest, and nothing has worked.

Worst part? The customer service is a total nightmare. I’ve spent HOURS chatting with different agents who make promises they don’t keep, repeat the same questions over and over, and keep “escalating” my case to nowhere. It’s been over a week. They’re replacing one pump, but won’t replace the other because of a firmware issue even though the app won’t let me update it, which is probably part of the same defect.

When I asked for a contact email or someone higher up, the rep refused. Flat out refused to give me a way to escalate and then asked me to be patient. Yep, you read that right!

I’m mentally and physically drained from dealing with this. I’m trying to feed my baby and instead, I’m fighting my pumps every single day while they refuse to take an ounce of accountability. I will NEVER give Willow my business again.
