I am quite embarrassed to take the bouncer anywhere.

I contacted customer services who agreed to the fault and accepted my claim under warranty. They have asked me to destroy the cover and send evidence of this before they can send another cover to me. It can take up to two weeks to receive the new cover which means I will be left without a bouncer for that length of time.

I have been advised it is their strict policy that all faulty products have to be destroyed immediately and proof sent to them for the claim to be approved. Whilst I appreciate the reasoning for this, in my case the issue is not of safety, but rather it is an aesthetic one. Therefore I cannot comprehend why a new cover cannot be sent straight away. I can then send evidence of this cover being destroyed.

To me that is not acceptable and it definitely is not something i am willing to compromise on when it comes to my baby and her needs.

The bouncer itself is not cheap, I expected a more understanding response and better customer service.

Do not buy the jersey fabric cover unless you want to be disappointed
