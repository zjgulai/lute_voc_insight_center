[## Disappointing product and customer service](https://www.trustpilot.com/reviews/69b037047750177dd4e530d7)

I am disappointed to be writing such a negative review but feel I have been left with no choice. As a first-time mum, buying a buggy felt like a big decision, particularly given it is such a significant investment. We chose the Bugaboo Dragonfly because we trusted the brand to deliver on quality of both product and service. Sadly, neither has been true in our experience.

The Bugaboo Dragonfly we purchased towards the end of 2025 would have been a great buggy, except for a major design flaw in the front wheels. They collect and trap debris so easily that the buggy became almost unusable, as the wheels were constantly blocked. On a damp day they needed to be unclogged every few metres. For a product that costs close to £1000, I expected far better.

Bugaboo, clearly aware that this issue was widespread, has since redesigned the wheels.

Given this, I assumed Bugaboo would make it straightforward for customers to receive the updated wheels. Unfortunately, that was not our experience.

Initially, Bugaboo suggested we wait several months for replacement wheels. When I explained this was not a workable solution as we could barely use the buggy anymore, they offered to send a replacement unit — but required me to remove the wheels and return the rest of the buggy. When I asked for a courier collection for the 10kg+ package, they initially refused because my postcode was close to a post office. How they expected me to carry a boxed buggy and a newborn to the post office is still beyond me. I was especially shocked given the reason for this return was because my buggy was not up to Bugaboo’s own standards !

They eventually agreed to arrange a courier collection. I stayed home all day waiting, but the courier never arrived. When I contacted customer service for an update, I received no response until the next day. They’ve since attempted to reschedule the courier for a day when I am not available (and requires me to stay home all day as they can’t provide a time slot in advance).

Overall, I am extremely disappointed with both the quality of the product and the follow-up customer service. At such a premium price point, I would not recommend purchasing from Bugaboo.
