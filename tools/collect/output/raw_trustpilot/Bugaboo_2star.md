Disappointed.

EDIT: 

The retailer said that the issue with the rivet cannot be put down as a claim - we must have damaged it ourselves. Without any inspection, based on a single picture. However - as a gesture of good will, they immediatelly added, they will replace the part for free.

I guess I should be happy, but the truth is - I think they are only leveraging the desperate position of us parents without a pram and fear that if we would go down with this as a regular claim, we would be granted by the law to ask for our money back completely, as there is already multiple claims per a single product.

Thus they are refusing the claim, but doing a "favor", which is not legally binding in any way for them.

I am 98% sure we did not cause the issue, but I need to be using the pram as soon as possible, so I accepted this resolution.

But I feel bitter rather than happy. 

Trust is broken - cannot recommend to purchase the pram to anyone at the moment.
